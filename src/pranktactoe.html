Prank Tac Toe
ios
My first attempt at a working app. It started when I competed in my school’s apps and analytics challenge that was hosted by microsoft. The rules were simple: Build a windows phone app and get the most downloads before the deadline. So I made a simple jump scare app called Uber Cat. You helped a cat jump over obstacles and after a certain amount of time a scary image would pop on the screen screaming at you. It was meant for a fun way to scare your friends. I ended up winning the event and decided to develop the app for ios. I dumped the cat for tic tac toe and implemented a really cool recording feature. So that when your friend would jump back from the scare the camera would record the reaction in the background. It’s done pretty well and the app store is now plaugued with similar jump scare apps.
